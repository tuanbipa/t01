Calendar View - Helpful Hints

  • Tap on the date bar to drop down a Mini Week view, then
  • Tap on the -> <- icon to toggle Mini Month view.
  • In either view, tap any date to navigate.
  • “Heat Map” shows busy days as dark.
  • Drag any Event into the Mini Month to reschedule.
  • Drag any Task into the Mini Month to change its deadline.
  • Drag the shaded box on either end of the calendar to change your working hours (graphic).
  • Tap any item to view its Notes, or to start typing or dictating.
  • Tap/Hold the time-line to create a new Event.
  • Select any item then drag its edges to change its duration.
