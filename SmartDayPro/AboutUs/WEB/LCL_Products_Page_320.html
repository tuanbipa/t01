Smart Pad
The Zen of Productivity

SmartPad is a friendly organizer for your iPad.  It integrates Task organization, Calendaring, Notes, Project planning, Time management and Recurring lists, all in one app. Patented logic technologies do everything for you behind the scenes while you stay organized and connected.

SmartCal
An integrated calendar and planner

SmartCal is the iPhone companion to SmartPad. The all-new SmartCal 3 features the "Thumb manager" with pull-down week or month calendars for quick navigation and drag/drop planning at the tip of your thumb. Built-in adaptive logic allows you to assign different working hours for each day of the week, then see and plan out your tasks and projects for the next 7 days. Changes that you make, or as time passes, update automatically.

SmartTime™ Organizer
An adaptive organizer with calendar

SmartTime features patented logic that looks at your appointments then finds time for your tasks. Three different views each integrate events and tasks, but the most unique is SmartView - an amazingly simple, intuitive list that follows the flow of your day. You have to try it to believe it. SmartTime integrates with your iPhone for dialing and texting, and also connects to your Contacts list and Google Maps. SmartTime users can even send "smart" tasks to each other!
